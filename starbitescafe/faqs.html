What is Star Bites Cafe?

Star Bites Cafe is a restaurant franchise available in locations all across the Outer Rim! You may have heard of Dex's Diner on Coruscant, or maybe Lothal's Bistro de Lothal during your travels. But with Star Bites Cafe, we make ourselves available on several planets, that way we're just a few lightyears away from you at all times!

What kind of food is at Star Bites Cafe?

Star Bites Cafe has many different foods! Ranging from fruit salads with rare Meilooruns, to meats, such as our Roasted Porg! In addition, Star Bites Cafe also sells special drinks to help on those hot days, such as Blue Milk or Batuubucha Tea!

What does is the environment at Star Bites Cafe like?

Star Bites Cafe is available to citizens of all races and ages. And unlike cantinas throughout the galaxy, we do serve droids at our cafe! Our restaurant interior is always clean and especially suited to each planet's atmosphere.
